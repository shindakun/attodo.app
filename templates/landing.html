What permissions does AT Todo request?

AT Todo requests the following permissions to function:

  • Task Storage - Store and manage your todos in your AT Protocol repository
  • List Storage - Create and manage task lists in your repository
  • Email Access - Read your email for account identification (optional)

Your privacy matters: AT Todo only accesses task and list data. We never access your social posts, followers, or other Bluesky content. All data stays in your repository under your control.
